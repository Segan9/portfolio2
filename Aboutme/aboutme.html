About Me

Shane Egan ITB Student

Bio:

My name is Shane Egan. I was born 16th May 1997. I live in Beaumont, Dublin. The area is most notable for its hospital which is recognised throughout the country. The area also contains the famous pub Beaumont House. I have three older sisters, Jennifer, Michelle and Suzanne. As a child I attended the local primary school St. Fiachras.
I then transitioned to an all boys secondary school, St. Aidans CBS, located in Whitehall. It was in this period of my life that I discovered my profound interest in film and storytelling. This passion led me to pursue a creative career. I am currently studying Creative Digital Media in the Institute of Technology Blanchardstown.
